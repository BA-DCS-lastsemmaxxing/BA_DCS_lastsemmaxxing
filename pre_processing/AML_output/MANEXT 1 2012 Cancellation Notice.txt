MA-N-EXT 1/2012 (CANCELLATION NOTICE) 2016
28 January 2016
NOTICE TO FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS
MONETARY AUTHORITY OF SINGAPORE ACT, CAP. 186
PROHIBITION ON TRANSACTIONS WITH THE IRANIAN GOVERNMENT AND
WITH IRANIAN FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS
1. This Notice is issued pursuant to section 27B of the Monetary Authority of
Singapore Act (Cap. 186).
2. MAS Notice MA-N-EXT 1/2012 on “Prohibition on Transactions with the Iranian
Government and with Iranian Financial Institutions” dated 18 June 2012 is
cancelled with effect from 28 January 2016.
